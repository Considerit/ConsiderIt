Why doesn’t the cost of the tax increase match the total ten-year cost projection?

The estimated cost of the Business and Occupation (B&O) tax increase is $170 million, rounded to the nearest million as required by I-960. ESB 6635 also decreases other taxes. The $24,234,000 total includes those tax decreases.
